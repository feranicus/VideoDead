# Acceptable Use

VideoDead is provided for downloading content that you **own** or have the **right** to download, including:

- Your own recorded lectures, talks, and webinars.
- Telehealth or meeting recordings you are a party to and are permitted to retain.
- Public-domain or openly-licensed (e.g. Creative Commons) educational and medical material.
- Content where the rights-holder has granted you permission to download.

## What this tool does not do

- It does **not** circumvent DRM or paywalls. The underlying yt-dlp engine refuses DRM-protected streams (e.g. Netflix, Disney+, most paid streaming).
- It is **not** a means to infringe copyright or to violate a website's terms of service.

## Your responsibility

You are responsible for ensuring that your use complies with applicable copyright law, the terms of service of the source website, and any privacy or data-protection obligations (including patient confidentiality where relevant). When in doubt, obtain permission from the rights-holder, or do not download.
